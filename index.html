— Our Story

Born from a
Passion for Purity

Founded in 2018 in Paris, Lumière was born from a simple belief: luxury beauty should never compromise on integrity. Our founder, Élise Moreau, spent a decade as a research chemist before creating formulas that blend the best of science and nature.

Every Lumière product is Leaping Bunny certified, sustainably sourced, and produced in our carbon-neutral atelier in Grasse, the perfume capital of the world.
